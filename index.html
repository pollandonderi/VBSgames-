DAY 1: Fill It Up

Learning outcomes

  • The Love of God like water fills my heart

What will be needed

  • 4 Buckets of 10L each
  • Water
  • 2 carwash sponges
  • 2 1l Water bottles
  • For a tape measure, we will use a rope

Process 1

Set a row of 2 buckets and fill each bucket with water (keep in mind, the more water you put in the bucket, the longer the game will take). Then set a row of 2 empty buckets on the opposite side of your area (distance between depends on the size of the groups). Each group gets a car wash sponge. Line up each group’s parcipants spaced in between the bucket with water and the empty bucket. The point of the game is for the child next to the water bucket to soak the sponge and carry it to the next child and so on unl the last child who carries it to the empty bucket to squeeze out. The group to empty their bucket the fastest while retaining the most water wins!
